Welcome to Smart HealthCare Information Hub

This platform provides trusted health information, librarian support, and digital services for the community. Explore health topics, chat with our AI assistant, and book appointments with healthcare consultants.

We are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of our community by providing educational resources on healthy living, disease prevention, mental wellness, and much more.
Our vision is to be the leading health resource library in the digital age, offering a comprehensive information hub for informed health decisions.
  • Health-related educational resources
  • Interactive workshops and webinars
  • AI-powered chatbot for quick health inquiries
  • Appointment booking for consultations
  • Virtual consultations and self-care tools
