Library Policy

Welcome to the Smart HealthCare Information Hub. As a digital health library, we aim to provide accurate, secure, and accessible health-related resources to our users. Please read the following policy to understand your rights and responsibilities when using our platform.

1. Purpose

This library provides users with reliable digital resources, research articles, and AI-powered tools in the field of health sciences and information management. It aims to support students, professionals, and the public in accessing trustworthy healthcare knowledge.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Use the resources for educational, research, or informational purposes only.
  • Do not share login or access credentials with unauthorized users.
  • Respect intellectual property and copyright laws when accessing or sharing content.

3. Privacy & Data Protection

  • We do not collect sensitive personal information.
  • Feedback forms and chatbot data are only used to improve library services.
  • User activity may be anonymously tracked to enhance service experience.

4. AI Chatbot Policy

  • The AI chatbot provides instant support but does not replace professional medical advice.
  • Do not input personal medical conditions, names, or identification numbers
  • All conversations are confidential and not shared with third parties.

5. Acceptable Use

  • No spamming, hacking, or uploading of malicious software.
  • Do not post or submit offensive, misleading, or harmful content.
  • Access to services may be restricted if misuse is detected.

6. External Links & Resources

We may link to trusted third-party sources. However, we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Always review external site policies before use.

7. Service Availability

Our digital services are available 24/7. However, occasional downtime may occur due to updates or technical issues. We strive to minimize disruptions and notify users of any scheduled maintenance.

8. Updates to Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Users will be informed through announcements on the website.

9. Contact Us
